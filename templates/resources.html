Mental Health Resources

MentalHealth.gov

This site offers a wealth of information on mental health issues, including educational resources, treatment options, and strategies for maintaining mental wellness.

NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness)

NAMI provides education, advocacy, and support for individuals affected by mental illness. Their website includes resources for finding help, understanding mental health conditions, and connecting with local support groups.

Psychology Today

Psychology Today features a comprehensive directory of therapists, articles on various mental health topics, and a wealth of information about psychological theories and practices.

BetterHelp

BetterHelp connects individuals with licensed therapists via online messaging, video calls, and live chats. It's an accessible option for those seeking mental health support from the comfort of their homes.
